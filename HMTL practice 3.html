Favorite Hobby

What is it?

Traditional drawing, simple and practically free for creative liberty, pen and paper.

What does it contain

Nothing too special; it is just a good way to express your creativity on physical paper.

  1. Have an idea in your head.
  2. Get a pen/pencil and paper.
  3. Draw a thumbnail so you can quickly draft our your idea.
  4. Then draw out the basic shapes to break down complex forms.
  5. You will then draw out the details and complex nuances as you build up the image.

Why do I like it?

It is just easier to use and I don't need a digital setup to draw.

Here's a list of why I think traditional drawings are good:

  • Traditional drawings cannot really be stolen by AI unlike digital drawings.
  • It feels "grounded" and normal to draw with.
  • It is inexpensive and easier to learn to and adapt to for beginners.

I don't hate digital art nor digital users, I think they're fantastic and useful for modern art now.
